✅ Do’s and ❌ Don’ts of Trading

Do’s

  • Trade with logic, not emotion — patience pays more than prediction.
  • Learn how price reacts at key levels before risking real money.
  • Keep a trading journal to reflect on wins and mistakes.
  • Respect stop-loss like your safety belt — it saves you every time.
  • Focus on consistency, not jackpot profits.

Don’ts

  • Don’t trade just because the market is open — trade when there’s a setup.
  • Never double your lot size just to recover a loss.
  • Don’t chase every candle; understand the story behind it.
  • Avoid taking trades out of boredom or FOMO.
  • Never let one bad trade decide your trading journey.
